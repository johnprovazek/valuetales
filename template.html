A notice has also been sent out to let know that they have one week to make any adjustments to any of their email responses. This is a good time to look at the generated Google Doc and suggest any edits that they could make.

Due to the open-source nature of this project there may also be some issues with the Google Doc that require code fixes or manual fixes from your end during this time.

Another thing to remind you of is the parameters coverPhotoWeekOverride and googleDocLinkOverride.

coverPhotoWeekOverride can be setup to select a random image or a specific weeks image for the cover photo.

googleDocLinkOverride can be set to three options. Setting this value to "auto" will automatically build and send the book without your review. Setting this value to "skip" will skip sending the book to your recipient next week and leave this process for you to do manually. Setting this value to to a Google Doc link will use that Google Doc instead of the auto-generated one. This is helpful incase you need to make any manual adjustments to the Google Doc.
